On Monday, we continued building the arcade cabinet. We split into 3 groups, 1 to make the control box, 1 to fit the front panel to the machine, and one to continue working on the Raspberry Pi. We didn't use power tools tonight, instead we all had a go with some traditional hand drills. They all worked very well, but we were especially impressed by the hand brace for putting in screws. We had a lot of success, and a lot has been done.
