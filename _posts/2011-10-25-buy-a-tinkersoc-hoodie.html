How to buy

The TinkerSoc hoodies are now on sale at http://www.kentunion.co.uk/societies/TinkerSoc/. Just go to the left hand column and click "add to basket" for either the hoodie, t-shirt or both. Once you checkout you'll be asked for your sizes.
If you want one (and of course you want one!) make sure you order it quickly. We're going to be sending out a bulk order once we have enough orders from you, so make sure you encourage your friends to get one!

Personalisation

After discussion with our suppliers, they agreed to give us the personalisation under the logo for free! To add your name or other personalisation make sure you fill out the form at http://goo.gl/Z8l9e, and we'll ensure that it gets added to your order.
